Services

Services

Expert consultancy and statutory compliance for industrial clients across Tamil Nadu & Puducherry.

Single Window System (Guidance Bureau)

Single point clearance for all industrial project approvals.

Directorate of Industrial Safety & Health (DISH)

Application 61Q, SAC documentation, Factory plan approval, License & Renewal, Contract labor registration & Amendments.

Tamilnadu Pollution Control Board

ETP/STP/RO/DI/Scrubber Consultancy, Consent to Establish/Operate, Hazardous Waste Authorization.

Department of Town & Country Planning Development

Plan preparation, Color coading, submission, Scrutiny payment, and plan approval coordination , BDO approval.

Petroleum & Explosives Safety Organization (PESO)

Petroleum / SMPV storage licensses, NOC from local authority, Amendments of licenses, Diesel tank erection & fabrication.

Tamilnadu Fire & Rescue Service Department

Fire license, Renewal, External fire audit, Fire Hydrant System as per NBC & Local Fire Department.

Tamilnadu Health & Preventive Medicine

Health plan approval, Suitability & Sanitary certificate, NOC, Methanol (DL2) & Poisons License, Solvent Order.

Tamilnadu Commissioner of Prohibition & Excise Department

Methanol License (DL2), coordination, reports, official endorsement, GO and proceeding order.

Tamilnadu Civil Supplies & Consumer Protection Department

Statutory compliance and documentation for industrial supply and consumer protection.

SIPCOT

Coordination, Documentation, and Statutory clearances for SIPCOT industrial projects.

Statutory Audits & Reports

Environmental, Safety, Fire, Electrical, Statutory compliance audits, Risk assessment, Job safety analysis, Accident Investigation.
